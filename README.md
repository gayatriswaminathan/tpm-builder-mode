# TPM Builder Mode

A program-management operating kit — fourteen governance mechanics translated into ready-to-use TPM templates, registers, and playbooks.

The through-line across everything here: **make the implicit explicit, force a verdict, and re-check whether each rule still earns its keep.**

## What's inside

| Path | Pattern(s) | Use it for |
|---|---|---|

| [`templates/go-no-go-gate.md`](templates/go-no-go-gate.md) | #1 | Launch gate with a forced verdict and one owner |

## Where to start
1. **Confidence badges** on your next status report (#2) — biggest signal-to-noise win for least effort.
2. **Forced verdicts** on your go/no-go gate (#1).
3. **Pre-register false positives** before your next launch (#6).
4. **Scored retro** after your next milestone (#3).
