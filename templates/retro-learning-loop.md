## When this runs
At a **boundary event** (pattern #9), not on a calendar: a phase gate crossed, a launch/ship, an incident, notable friction or a notable win, or a change to the process itself.

## 4. Feedback (close the loop at the next boundary)
For each promoted item whose re-check date has passed:
- **Delivered the predicted gain?** → keep it, mark `VALIDATED`.
- **Didn't?** → `SUNSET` it (remove/soften the guardrail). A rule that costs friction without paying for it is bloat. (Pattern #12.)
